# Abstract

CrackNJ156 Segmentation is a curated packaging of a manually annotated pavement crack dataset released on Zenodo with the LETNet publication.

The associated article presents a locally enhanced Transformer network for high-resolution crack detection and describes image collection across multiple pavement materials, weather and season conditions, and both natural and street-light illumination. This package standardizes metadata, statistics, and visualization artifacts for the Large Road Damage DataLake.
